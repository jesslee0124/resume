About Me

I am a freelance front-end developer based in Toronto. I love developing simple websites with clear code. Converting beautiful designs into live and functional websites gives me joy. I work with HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and WordPress. For version control, I use Github and Bitbucket.

I am a graduate from Sheridan College with Diploma in Computer Programmer with high-honours. Previously, I finished Bachelor of Arts in English Language and Literature and used to teach English at a local university in South Korea. I decided to study programming while researching ways to build a custom curriculum. When I found myself spending more time on coding than preparing for classes, I realized I had to pursue my new passion.

When I am not coding, I love listening to music as well as playing the piano. I enjoy learning about new cultures and languages. I am also a huge coffee lover.
